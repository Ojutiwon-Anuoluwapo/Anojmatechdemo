About ANOJMATech

We deliver practical training, useful products, and hands-on consulting to help people and organisations succeed with everyday technology.

Who we are

ANOJMATech is a small technology company focused on building simple, practical tools, offering tailored consultancy, and delivering accessible training. Our goal is to increase digital confidence and productivity across communities and small businesses.

MicroSavvy — Our training arm

MicroSavvy is the training sub‑brand of ANOJMATech that specialises in Microsoft-focused courses and digital literacy. We run hands-on workshops and short courses designed for learners at all levels.

Products & Consulting

Beyond training, ANOJMATech builds small-scale productivity tools and offers consulting to streamline workflows and improve outcomes for organisations with limited technical resources.

Community & Scholarships

We run community programs and occasionally offer partial scholarships for our courses to increase access. If you or your organisation needs support, please contact us or register your interest on the homepage.
